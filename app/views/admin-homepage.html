What do high, medium and low ratings mean?

  • Risk:
    • High : probability of collision over 1%
    • Medium : probability of collision over 0.1%
    • Low : probability of collision below 0.1%
  • Impact:
    • Defined by member of the UKSA SST team based on potential impact to UK assets and services.

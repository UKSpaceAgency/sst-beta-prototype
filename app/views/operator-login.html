Setting up your account

Welcome to 'Monitor my satellites'. Before you start using this application, please define your account settings.

Set your event status and notification thresholds:

  • Conjunction events can be categorised as high, medium or low events.
  • For each category you need to define thresholds based on four criteria.
  • An event status is listed as high or medium if any of your specified thresholds are met.
  • Any events below these thresholds will be defined as low.
  • Leave criteria you do not wish to be considered blank.

Help with event status thresholds
Event status thresholds will depend on your risk tolerances, however common thresholds for event status may include:

  • Probability of collision: a high risk event may have a probability over 1%; medium over 0.1%; and low below 0.1%.
  • Miss distances: a high risk event may have a miss distance below 500m; medium below 1 km; and low over 1 km.
  • Time to event: this will depend on how much warning you would like about an event and how long you need to plan a response.

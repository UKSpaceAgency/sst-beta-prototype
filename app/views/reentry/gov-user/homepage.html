Help with this table
This table shows all upcoming and previous re-entry events that have been identified by Space-Track and ingested into Monitor space hazards, including events which pose a risk to the UK. Higher risk events contain additional analysis by UKSA's Orbital Analysts at the National Space Operations Centre.

  • Select the Event ID to view more information
  • Each Event ID or row represents one re-entry event including all related alert updates
  • Sort the alerts according to criteria by clicking on the relevant headings

Alert thresholds

Alerts about re-entry events are produced if:

  • Less than 36 hours until re-entry
  • AND one of the following:
    • Any “large” object in US SATCAT (except designed for demise) with overflights of the UK mainland
    • OR any “large” object in US SATCAT (except designed for demise) with more than 0.1% chance of impact in overseas territories
    • OR any UK-licensed object (except designed for demise)
    • OR (anticipated) press interest
  • OR nominated for escalation by the National Space Operations Centre

Risk thresholds

Risk thresholds are calculated according to the probability of the object re-entering over the UK.

Risk level Probability of re-entry over the UK (%)

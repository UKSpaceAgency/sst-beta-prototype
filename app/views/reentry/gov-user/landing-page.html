This service tracks all upcoming re-entry events worldwide, including those which pose a risk to the UK. The service reports on two types of re-entry event.

  • Upcoming re-entry alerts: all upcoming re-entry events that meet the UK Space Agency's thresholds for alerting UK government
  • Upcoming re-entry events worldwide: all upcoming re-entry events worldwide, including those which have not met alerting thresholds

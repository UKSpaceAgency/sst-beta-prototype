Event summary


Flagged object: Hylas 1

UKSA's orbital analysis team are reporting on a manoeuvre by Hylas 1 that exceeds current tolerance values for its reporting criteria. Between the 16th and 18th of January 2024 it raised its orbit by approximately 225 km.

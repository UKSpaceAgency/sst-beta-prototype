Higher risk events contain additional analysis by UKSA's Orbital Analysts at the National Space Operations Centre.

  • Sort the alerts according to criteria by clicking on the relevant headings

Alert thresholds

Alerts about fragmentation events are produced if:

  • there is an increased risk of a potential collision with a UK operated satellites or Human Spaceflight mission
  • OR nominated for escalation by the National Space Operations Centre

Risk thresholds

Risk thresholds are calculated according to the probability of the object affecting UK operated satellites or Human Spaceflight mission.

Risk level Increase in potential collision with UK satellites (%)
Low Less than 1%
Medium 1% - 5%

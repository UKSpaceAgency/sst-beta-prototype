Alert and risk thresholds

Alert thresholds

Alerts about conjunction events are produced if:

  • Less than 5 days until Time of Closest Approach
  • AND for UK-licensed objects:
    • More than 0.1% probability of collision
    • OR press interest
  • AND for non-UK objects:
    • Low risk or higher
    • AND no manoeuvre expected
  • OR nominated for escalation by the UK Space Operations Centre

Risk thresholds

Risk thresholds are calculated accounting for the probability of the collision between the two objects (as calculated by the US), and the average number of additional conjunction events that the collision would create for UK satellites.

Any event with an expected maneouvre is automatically categorised as "Low risk".

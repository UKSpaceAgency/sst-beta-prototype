This service tracks all upcoming conjunction events for UK licenced objects. The service reports on two types of conjunction event.

  • Upcoming conjunction alerts: all upcoming conjunction events that meet the UK Space Agency's thresholds for alerting UK government
  • Upcoming conjunction events: all upcoming conjunction events, including those which have not met alerting thresholds, either for a specific operator or all UK-licenced satellites

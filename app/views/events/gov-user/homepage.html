Risk and escalation thresholds

Escalation criteria

Conjunction events are escalated if:

  • The object is UK-licensed or owned, the probability of collision is greater than 0.1% and the time of closest approach is within 5 days
  • The event detail criteria for 'Low Risk' are met
  • There is press or government interest in the event

Risk thresholds

Risk thresholds are calculated accounting for the probability of the collision between the two objects (as calculated by the US), and the average number of additional conjunction events that the collision would create for UK satellites.

Any event with an expected maneouvre is automatically categorised as "Low risk".

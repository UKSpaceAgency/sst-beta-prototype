Monitor Space Hazards

National Space Operations Centre Warning and Protection Services

National Space Operations Centre (NSpOC) warning and protection services support UK and international partners to manage space related threats, risks and hazards.

Use this service to:

  • Track conjunction events
  • Track re-entry events
  • Track UK satellite positions
  • View and download satellite history
  • Explore key statistics for UK space hazards

If you are a registered user, you can sign in by entering your email address and password on the next page.

If you are not a registered user, but would like to use this service, contact monitorspacehazards@ukspaceagency.gov.uk.

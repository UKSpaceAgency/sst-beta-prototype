AAA0001: Evolution of error ellipsoids

This plot shows the expected miss distance for each of the event CDMs from oldest to newest. The plot turns a 3D miss distance into a 2D plot with the radial miss distance (altitude separation) on the y axis and the along track and normal miss distance combined onto the x-axis. The expected 1 σ uncertainties at the TCA are then shown as an oval around the measured miss distance – there is a 60% chance that the actual miss distance lies within that oval.

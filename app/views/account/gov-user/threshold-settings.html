Escalating to the early distribution list

The early distribution list is notified of all escalated conjunction events. These are events which meet the following criteria:

  • <5 days until Time of Closest Approach
  • AND for UK-licensed objects:
    • 0.1% probability of collision
    • OR press interest
  • AND for non-UK objects:
    • Low risk or higher
    • AND no manoeuvre expected
  • OR nominated for escalation by the UK Space Operations Centre
Escalating to the extended distribution list

The early distribution list is only notified of the highest priority escalated conjunction events. These are events which meet the following criteria in addition to all of the criteria above:

  • >5% Probability of Collision
  • OR significant press interest
  • OR for UK-licensed objects:
    • >1% probability of collision
    • AND no manoeuvre expected
  • OR for non-UK objects:
    • Medium risk or higher
    • AND no manoeuvre expected

View criteria for escalating to the early distribution list and extended distribution list
Escalating to the early distribution list

The early distribution list is notified of all escalated conjunction events. These are events which meet the following criteria:

  • <36 hours until re-entry
  • AND one of the following:
    • Any “large” object in US SATCAT (except designed for demise) with overflights of the UK mainland
    • OR any “large” object in US SATCAT (except designed for demise) with >0.1% chance of impact in overseas territories
    • OR any UK-licensed object (except designed for demise)
    • OR (anticipated) press interest
  • OR nominated for escalation by the UK Space Operations Centre
Escalating to the extended distribution list

The early distribution list is only notified of the highest priority escalated conjunction events. These are events which meet the following criteria in addition to all of the criteria above:

  • Debris likely to survive to the ground
  • AND one of the following:
    • >0.1% chance of impact in the UK (and overseas territories on case by case basis)
    • OR UK-licensed object
    • OR significant press interest

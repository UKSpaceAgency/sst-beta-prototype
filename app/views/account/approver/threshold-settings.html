Recommended for all other government departments

Criteria for priority conjunction alerts

Priority conjunction alerts are those with:

  • More than 5% Probability of Collision
  • OR significant press interest
  • OR for UK-licensed objects:
    • More than 1% probability of collision
    • AND no manoeuvre expected
  • OR for non-UK objects:
    • Medium risk or higher
    • AND no manoeuvre expected

Select one option.
Recommended for UK Space Agency, Civil Aviation Authority, Ministry of Defence and international partners
Recommended for all other government departments
Criteria for priority re-entry alerts

Priority re-entry alerts are those with:

  • Debris likely to survive to the ground
  • AND one of the following:
    • More than 0.1% chance of impact in the UK (and overseas territories on case by case basis)
    • OR UK-licensed object
    • OR significant press interest

Satellite activity notification settings

This page allows you to adjust your notification settings for satellite activity.

You can create universal settings which will apply to all satellites.

You can also create custom settings which will only apply to selected satellites. For those selected satellites, your custom settings will not cancel out your universal settings. You will receive both sets of notifications.

We therefore recommend that your universal settings should be less inclusive than your custom settings.

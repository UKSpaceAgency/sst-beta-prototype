Contact a UKSA analyst

UKSA analysts are available Monday to Sunday, 9am to 5pm (except public holidays). Your message will be triaged and an analyst will get back to you as soon as possible.

UKSA analysts can provide:
  • Further detail on their calculations
  • An indication on whether further analysis of an event is possible
UKSA analysts cannot provide:
  • Advice on how you should manoeuvre your satellite
  • Analysis on a planned manoeuvre

Monitor space hazards

National Space Operations Centre Warning and Protection Services

National Space Operations Centre (NSpOC) warning and protection services support UK and international partners to manage space related threats, risks and hazards.


View all UK licensed satellites

  • View information on UK licensed satellites, including operator, position, launch information, and upcoming events.

Track conjunction events

  • Track all conjunction events involving UK satellites with NSpOC's In-Space Collision Avoidance System. Access further detail on specific events, view probability of collision, miss-distances and other key metrics, and extract raw data.

Track re-entry events

  • Track all re-entry events over the UK and British Overseas Territories with NSpOC's Uncontrolled Re-Entry Early Warning System. Access further detail on specific events, assess risk to life and property on the ground, and extract raw data.


Track UK satellite positions

  • Track the positions and movements of all UK-licensed satellites with NSpOC's UK Satellite Tracking Service. Access further details about position changes through monitoring flags, and extract raw data.

Check space weather

  • View space weather alerts from the Met Office Space Weather Operations Centre (MOSWOC).

Explore key statistics

  • View and explore key statistics related to the data ingested by the Monitor space hazards service, including data broken down by organisation and satellite.


Change account settings

  • Change notification settings by adapting your alert thresholds, select your areas of interest for re-entry events, choose your preferred method for receiving alerts, view T&Cs and see account details.

Access support and guidance

  • Access support for using the service, uploading and managing ephermeris data and using the API, including a demonstration video.
